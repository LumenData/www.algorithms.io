Pricing FAQ

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored in Rackspace. Ingesting data into the database is free. Storage pricing is included with the monthly price and data is stored for the length of the contract. If you choose to end your contract data is available for export within 10 days.

Is my data secure?
All data is secured with SSL encryption.

What algorithms are included?
The algorithm catalog includes neural network, decision tree, random forest, support vector machine, linear regression and anomaly detection algorithms. The Algorithms.io model builder identifies the best fit with with data automatically. Algorithms.io is available to help with custom algorithm development and implementation.

What happens if I exceed the data points allowed?
Additional data is charged at $1/million data points
